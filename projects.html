STACK RUNNER

Technologies: MEAN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, Node.js), Ng Bootstrap,

Description: STACK RUNNER is a powerful testing tool specifically designed to streamline the testing of Inteuition APIs. Given its vast range of business scenarios, developing and testing even minor changes in Inteuition can be incredibly time-consuming. Often, the time spent on testing exceeds the time taken for actual development.
